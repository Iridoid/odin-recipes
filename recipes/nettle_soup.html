Kornmjölsgröt (Barley Porridge)

Description

Nässelsoppa is a well-known sign of spring in Sweden with hard-boiled eggs and crème fraîche as traditional accompaniments. Packed with nutrition and flavour, nettles have high quantities of iron, calcium, vitamin A and K. In medieval Europe they were used medicinally as a diuretic and to treat joint pain. Early nettles are tender and perfect for cooking.

Ingredients

  • 15 cups Water
  • Salt
  • 2 cups barley kernels ; chopped, soaked overnight in cold water
  • 1 Handful Spelt flour
  • 1 Handful Hazelnuts ; Crushed
  • 3 tablespoons Honey

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients in a large pot;
  2. Pour 10 cups of water in the kettle and heat to a rolling boil;
  3. Stir regularly, reducing heat if needed to maintain a low boil;
  4. Add water if needed if the mixture starts getting too thick;
  5. Cook for about an hour until done.
